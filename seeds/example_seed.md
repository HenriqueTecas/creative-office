# Seed: The Body as Platform

## Domain
Products and services that treat human biological data as a medium
for new experiences, not just health monitoring.

## Temporal Frame
Buildable by 2032. Uses technology that exists today or is in
late-stage research. No brain-computer interfaces, no nanotech,
no sci-fi handwaving.

## Constraints
- Every idea must have a first customer who isn't a tech enthusiast.
  Normal people need to want this.
- Nothing that's primarily a medical device (regulatory death sentence
  for startups).
- Must involve data that flows between people, not just individual
  self-tracking.

## Provocation
The quantified self movement failed because it made the body into
a dashboard. What if instead of reading your body like a spreadsheet,
you could use your biological signals as an instrument — something
that creates, communicates, or connects rather than just reports?

## Anti-targets
- "AI-powered health app" — boring, done, saturated
- Anything that's basically a Fitbit with extra steps
- Pure wellness/meditation products
- Anything that requires the user to look at a screen
