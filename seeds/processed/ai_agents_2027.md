# Seed: AI Agents That Work For You (Not The Other Way Around)

## Domain
Consumer and prosumer AI agents that genuinely reduce cognitive load — not another chatbot, not another assistant that requires management. Agents that act autonomously within clear boundaries and make your life simpler without creating new work.

## Temporal Frame
Buildable by 2027. Uses current LLM capabilities, existing APIs, and commodity infrastructure. No AGI, no brain-computer interfaces, no sci-fi.

## Constraints
- Must work with existing tools (email, calendar, banking, shopping, travel)
- Privacy-first: local processing where possible, clear data boundaries
- Must have a clear "off ramp" — humans can always override or disable
- No surveillance capitalism business models

## Provocation
Every "AI assistant" today creates more work: you manage it, correct it, review its suggestions, configure its settings. What if an AI agent was more like a trusted employee than a tool? It has a job description, clear authority limits, and you evaluate outcomes — not every action.

## Anti-targets
- "AI-powered [existing app]" — wrapping old software in chat doesn't help
- Another chatbot interface
- Agents that require constant supervision
- Anything that sells your data or attention

## Your Angle
Europe-first: GDPR-native, privacy-preserving, no surveillance business model. Target users who are skeptical of Big Tech but see the value in automation. Think "Swiss bank account for your digital life" — trustworthy, private, effective.

## Themes to Explore
- **Delegation vs. control** — What should agents decide alone vs. escalate?
- **Trust calibration** — How do agents earn and maintain trust?
- **Multi-agent coordination** — Your agent negotiating with other agents (airline, bank, vendor)
- **Failure modes** — What happens when agents disagree or make mistakes?
- **Economic models** — How do you pay for agents without selling data?
